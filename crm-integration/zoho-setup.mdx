# Zoho CRM Setup

This guide walks you through authenticating your Zoho CRM account and connecting it to Indigenius.

---

## Prerequisites

- An active Zoho CRM account (Standard plan or above)
- Zoho CRM Administrator access
- Your Indigenius account on a **Prime** or **Enterprise** plan

<Note>
  CRM integration is not available on the **Cool** or **Free Trial** plans. See [Commercials](/commercials/plans) to upgrade.
</Note>

---

## Step 1 — Open Integrations

1. Log in to your [Indigenius Dashboard](https://agent.indigenius.ai/dashboard)
2. Click **Integrations** in the left sidebar
3. Click the **Zoho CRM** tile
4. Click **Connect Zoho CRM**

---

## Step 2 — Authenticate with OAuth

1. You'll be redirected to the Zoho login page
2. Log in with your Zoho CRM Administrator credentials
3. On the permissions screen, review the requested scopes:
   - `ZohoCRM.modules.leads.ALL` — read and write access to Leads
   - `ZohoCRM.modules.contacts.ALL` — read and write access to Contacts
   - `ZohoCRM.settings.fields.READ` — read field definitions
4. Click **Accept**
5. You'll be redirected back to Indigenius with a **✅ Connected** status

<Warning>
  Do NOT refresh the page during the OAuth redirect flow. If the connection fails, return to Integrations and click **Reconnect**.
</Warning>

---

## Step 3 — Configure Integration Settings

After connecting:

1. Choose your **Primary Module**: `Leads` or `Contacts` (or both)
2. Set your **Sync Direction**:
   - `Zoho → Indigenius only` — contacts flow into Indigenius but outcomes are NOT written back
   - `Bidirectional` — full two-way sync (recommended)
3. Set **Sync Frequency**:
   - `Real-time` — new records in Zoho trigger immediate sync (webhook-based)
   - `Every 1 hour`
   - `Daily at midnight`
4. Click **Save Settings**

---

## Step 4 — Verify the Connection

1. In Zoho CRM, create a test Lead with a valid Nigerian phone number
2. Return to Indigenius **→ Contacts**
3. The test contact should appear within 60 seconds (real-time) or at next sync
4. If it doesn’t appear, click **Integrations → Zoho → Sync Now** to trigger a manual sync

---

## Disconnecting Zoho CRM

1. Go to **Integrations → Zoho CRM**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the action

<Warning>
  Disconnecting pauses all CRM-triggered automations and contact syncs. Existing contacts in Indigenius are retained, but no new ones will be added until you reconnect.
</Warning>

---

## Troubleshooting

**Connection failed during OAuth.** Ensure you’re logged into Zoho as an Administrator, not a regular user. Only Admins can grant API access.

**Contacts not syncing.** Check that the sync direction is set to `Bidirectional` or `Zoho → Indigenius`. Also verify that your contacts have a valid phone number field populated.

**"Invalid token" error.** Zoho OAuth tokens expire after 60 minutes of inactivity. Go to **Integrations → Zoho** and click **Refresh Token**.
