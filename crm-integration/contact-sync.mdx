# Contact Synchronisation

Contact sync keeps your Indigenius contact lists up to date with your Zoho CRM records — automatically.

---

## Initial Sync

After [connecting Zoho CRM](/crm-integration/zoho-setup) and [mapping fields](/crm-integration/field-mapping), run your initial sync:

1. Go to **Integrations → Zoho CRM → Sync**
2. Click **Run Full Sync**
3. Choose which module to sync: **Leads**, **Contacts**, or **Both**
4. Apply filters if needed (e.g., only sync `Lead Status = New`)
5. Click **Start Sync**

The sync progress is shown in real-time. Large lists (10,000\+ contacts) may take 2–5 minutes.

<Tip>
  For your first sync, apply a filter to import only **active or recent** records. Avoid importing your entire CRM history, as this can create noise in your campaigns.
</Tip>

---

## Sync Filters

You can filter which records sync from Zoho:

| Filter Type | Example |
| --- | --- |
| By Status | `Lead Status = New` or `Lead Status = Contacted` |
| By Date | `Created Date is after 01/01/2025` |
| By Source | `Lead Source = Website` |
| By Owner | `Lead Owner = [Your Name]` |
| By Tag | Custom Zoho tags |

---

## Real-Time Sync

With real-time sync enabled, new Zoho records are added to Indigenius immediately via **webhooks**:

1. Go to **Integrations → Zoho → Settings**
2. Set **Sync Mode: Real-Time**
3. Indigenius registers a webhook in your Zoho account automatically
4. From this point, every new Lead/Contact in Zoho appears in Indigenius within seconds

---

## Scheduled Sync

For less real-time needs, use scheduled sync:

- **Every 1 hour** — good for active campaigns
- **Every 6 hours** — balanced performance
- **Daily at midnight** — low-overhead option for batch campaigns

---

## Handling Duplicates

Indigenius uses **phone number** as the unique identifier to detect duplicates.

| Scenario | Behaviour |
| --- | --- |
| Same phone, different name | Keeps the most recent record |
| Same phone, different email | Merges the records |
| Exact duplicate | Skipped silently |

---

## Deleting Synced Contacts

If a contact is deleted from Zoho:

- With **bidirectional sync**: the contact is archived in Indigenius (not deleted)
- To fully delete: go to **Contacts**, search for the record, and click **Delete**
