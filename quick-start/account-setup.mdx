# Account Setup

This page covers everything you need to know about creating and configuring your Indigenius account after signing up.

---

## Creating Your Account

1. Go to [https://agent.indigenius.ai/signup](https://agent.indigenius.ai/signup)
2. Fill in your **Full Name**, **Work Email**, and a strong **Password**
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Open your email inbox and click the **Verify Email** link
5. You'll be redirected to the dashboard automatically

<Warning>
  The verification link expires after **24 hours**. If it expires, return to the login page and request a new verification email.
</Warning>

---

## Workspace Settings

After logging in, configure your workspace:

1. Click your **Profile icon** (top right) → **Settings**
2. Update your **Company Name** — this appears in agent call logs
3. Set your **Timezone** — important for scheduling outbound campaigns correctly
4. Upload a **Company Logo** if desired
5. Click **Save Changes**

---

## Inviting Team Members

You can invite colleagues to collaborate on your Indigenius workspace:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter their email address and choose a **Role**:
   - **Admin** — full access including billing
   - **Manager** — can create agents and campaigns, cannot change billing
   - **Viewer** — read-only access to logs and reports
4. Click **Send Invite**

<Tip>
  Team members receive an email invitation. They must create their own password on first login.
</Tip>

---

## Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings → Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password, then your new password twice
4. Click **Update Password**

---

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA:

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Two-Factor Authentication**
2. Click **Enable 2FA**
3. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm
5. Save your **backup codes** in a secure location

---

## Troubleshooting

**I didn't receive a verification email.** Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, return to the login page and click **Resend Verification Email**.

**I forgot my password.** Click **Forgot Password** on the login page. A reset link will be sent to your email.

**My verification link is expired.** Return to [https://agent.indigenius.ai/login](https://agent.indigenius.ai/login), log in with your credentials, and request a new verification email from the banner prompt.
