# Quick Start

Welcome to Indigenius. This guide walks you through the fastest path to getting your first Voice AI agent live — from account creation to your first test call.

<Note>
  **Estimated time:** 10–15 minutes. No coding required.
</Note>

## The Fastest Setup Path

Here's the full end-to-end flow at a glance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [agent.indigenius.ai](https://agent.indigenius.ai/signup) using your email address. Confirm your email and log in to the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your first agent">
    Navigate to **Agents** in the left sidebar and click **Create Agent**. Give your agent a name, a purpose, and a system prompt. See [Agent Setup](/agent-setup/overview) for full details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the website widget">
    Go to **Widget** in the sidebar. Copy the embed snippet and paste it before the `</body>` tag on your website. See [Website Widget](/AgentPro/Widget) for customization options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Zoho CRM">
    Go to **Integrations → Zoho CRM**, authenticate with OAuth, and map your contact fields. See [CRM Integration](/crm-integration/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload contacts">
    Navigate to **Contacts → Upload**. Download the CSV template, fill in your contacts, and upload. Contacts sync automatically to your CRM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test inbound calls">
    Purchase a phone number under **Phone Numbers**, assign your agent, and call the number from your mobile phone. See [Phone Setup](/phone-setup/overview).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test outbound calls">
    Go to **Campaigns → New Campaign**, select your contacts list, choose your agent, and run a test call to a single contact first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review call logs">
    Open **Logs** in the sidebar to review transcripts, durations, and outcomes. Use this to identify gaps in your agent's responses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize performance">
    Refine your agent's prompt, update your knowledge base, and adjust branching logic based on what you see in the logs. See [Testing Checklist](/testing/checklist).
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A valid email address for account signup
- Admin access to your website (to embed the widget)
- A Zoho CRM account (for CRM integration — optional at this stage)
- A contact list in CSV format (for outbound campaigns)

---

## Step 1 — Create Your Account

1. Visit [https://agent.indigenius.ai/signup](https://agent.indigenius.ai/signup)
2. Enter your full name, email address, and password
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Check your inbox and click the verification link
5. Log in and you'll land on the **Dashboard**

<Tip>
  Use a work email address. Invoices and system notifications will be sent to this address.
</Tip>

---

## Step 2 — Explore the Dashboard

Once logged in, you'll see the main navigation on the left:

| Section | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Agents** | Create and manage your AI agents |
| **Phone Numbers** | Buy and configure numbers |
| **Widget** | Embed call widget on your website |
| **Campaigns** | Create and run outbound call campaigns |
| **Contacts** | Upload and manage contact lists |
| **Integrations** | Connect Zoho CRM and other tools |
| **Logs** | View call transcripts and outcomes |
| **Billing** | Manage your plan and usage |
