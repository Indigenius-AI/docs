# Appointment Booking Flow

The **Appointment Booking Flow** enables your AI agent to call contacts, check available slots, and confirm appointments — all within a single phone call.

---

## Prerequisites

- An agent configured with a booking-focused prompt
- A calendar integration (Google Calendar, Calendly, or manual slot management)
- A contact list with names and phone numbers
- SMS capability enabled on your account

---

## Sample Agent Prompt for Booking

```text
You are Kemi, a scheduling assistant for Acme Solar.
Your goal is to book a free 30-minute site survey for qualified solar leads.

Available appointment slots (use these exactly):
- Monday to Friday: 9am, 11am, 2pm, 4pm
- Saturday: 10am, 12pm

Process:
1. Confirm you're speaking with [Lead Name]
2. Say: "Great news — you've been approved for a free solar site survey! I'd love to book a convenient time for one of our engineers to visit."
3. Offer 2 available slots from the nearest 3 business days
4. Confirm the chosen slot and collect the property address
5. Say: "Perfect! I'll send a confirmation SMS right away. Is this the best number to reach you on?"
6. End the call warmly

If no slots work, offer to call back tomorrow with updated availability.
```

---

## Setting Up the Booking Flow

### Option A: Manual Slot List

1. Maintain a text list of available slots in your agent's prompt (updated daily or weekly)
2. Agent verbally offers slots and confirms via SMS after call
3. Best for small teams with predictable availability

### Option B: Calendar Integration

1. Go to **Integrations → Calendar**
2. Connect your **Google Calendar** or **Calendly** account
3. Set your **booking duration** (e.g., 30 minutes)
4. Set your **buffer time** between appointments (e.g., 15 minutes)
5. The agent pulls live availability and books directly into your calendar

---

## SMS Confirmation Setup

After every successful booking:

1. Go to **Campaigns →** your campaign **→ Post-Call Actions**
2. Enable **Send Booking Confirmation SMS**
3. Write your SMS template:

```text
Hi {{first_name}}, your Acme Solar site survey is confirmed for {{appointment_date}} at {{appointment_time}}.
Address: {{property_address}}.
Questions? Call us: {{company_phone}}.
```

---

## Handling Reschedule Requests

Add this to your agent prompt:

```text
If the caller asks to reschedule:
1. Apologise for the inconvenience
2. Offer 3 alternative slots
3. Confirm the new slot and resend the SMS confirmation
4. Update the CRM record with the new appointment time
```

---

## Tracking Bookings

Go to **Campaigns → Results** to see:

- Number of appointments booked
- No-show rate (after cross-referencing with calendar)
- Rescheduled appointments
- Calls that resulted in declined bookings
