# SMS & Email Follow-Up

After every call, Indigenius can automatically send **SMS messages** and **emails** to the contact based on the call outcome. This keeps your communication seamless and ensures no lead or customer falls through the cracks.

---

## How Post-Call Automations Work

```text
Call ends → Outcome logged → Automation triggers → SMS or email sent
```

Automations are triggered by **call outcomes** such as:

- Call connected and completed
- Appointment booked
- Lead qualified or unqualified
- Call unanswered (after retries exhausted)
- Customer expressed a complaint

---

## Setting Up SMS Follow-Up

1. Go to **Campaigns →** your campaign **→ Post-Call Actions**
2. Click **Add SMS Action**
3. Choose a **Trigger Outcome** (e.g., `Appointment Booked`)
4. Write your SMS message using template variables:

```text
Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for speaking with us!
Your appointment is confirmed for {{appointment_date}} at {{appointment_time}}.
Reply STOP to opt out.
— Acme Solar
```

5. Set the **Send Delay** (e.g., `Immediately` or `5 minutes after call ends`)
6. Save

### Available SMS Variables

| Variable | Description |
| --- | --- |
| `{{first_name}}` | Contact's first name |
| `{{last_name}}` | Contact's last name |
| `{{phone}}` | Contact's phone number |
| `{{appointment_date}}` | Booked appointment date |
| `{{appointment_time}}` | Booked appointment time |
| `{{agent_name}}` | Name of the AI agent |
| `{{company_name}}` | Your company name |
| `{{call_outcome}}` | Outcome code (e.g., QUALIFIED) |

---

## Setting Up Email Follow-Up

1. Go to **Campaigns →** your campaign **→ Post-Call Actions**
2. Click **Add Email Action**
3. Set the **Trigger Outcome**
4. Enter the **From Name** and **Reply-To Email**
5. Write your **Subject Line** and **Email Body**
6. Use HTML or the visual editor
7. Save

<Note>
  Email follow-ups require a verified sender domain. Go to **Settings → Email** to verify your domain via DNS records.
</Note>

## Sample SMS Templates

**Appointment Confirmation:**

```text
Hi {{first_name}}, your free solar survey is booked!
Date: {{appointment_date}} at {{appointment_time}}.
Our engineer will visit {{property_address}}.
Questions? Call {{company_phone}}. — Acme Solar
```

**Brochure for Warm Leads:**

```text
Hi {{first_name}}, thanks for your interest in Acme Solar.
Here's our product guide: https://acmesolar.ng/guide
We'll be in touch soon. — Acme Team
```

**Missed Call Notification:**

```text
Hi {{first_name}}, we tried to reach you from Acme Solar but couldn't connect.
We'll try again tomorrow, or you can call us on {{company_phone}}.
```

## Compliance & Opt-Out

<Warning>
  All SMS messages must include an **opt-out instruction** (e.g., "Reply STOP to unsubscribe"). Failure to include this may violate NCC and NDPR regulations in Nigeria, and CAN-SPAM / TCPA in the US.
</Warning>

Indigenius automatically handles opt-out responses:

- If a contact replies **STOP**, they are added to your **Do Not Contact** list
- Future campaigns exclude opted-out contacts automatically
