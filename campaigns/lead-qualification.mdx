# Lead Qualification Flow

The **Lead Qualification Flow** uses your AI agent to call leads, ask structured qualification questions, score their responses, and route them appropriately — all without human involvement unless a lead meets your criteria.

---

## How It Works

```text
Lead submits form → Trigger call (manual or CRM) → Agent calls lead
         ↓
   Agent asks qualification questions
         ↓
   Lead scores High / Medium / Low
         ↓
   High  → Book appointment
   Medium → SMS follow-up + add to nurture list
   Low   → Log as unqualified
```

---

## Step-by-Step Setup

### 1. Define Your Qualification Criteria

Before building the flow, decide what makes a lead qualified. Example:

| Question | Qualified Answer |
| --- | --- |
| Are you the decision-maker? | Yes |
| Monthly electricity bill? | Above ₦30,000 |
| Property type? | Residential or Commercial |
| Ready to install within 3 months? | Yes |

### 2. Write the Agent Prompt

Add a qualification scoring instruction to your agent's prompt:

```text
You are qualifying leads for Acme Solar. Ask these questions in order:
1. "Are you the property owner or decision-maker for energy purchases?"
2. "What is your average monthly electricity bill?"
3. "Is your property residential or commercial?"
4. "Are you looking to install within the next 3 months?"

Score the lead:
- All 4 answered positively = HOT LEAD → offer to book a site survey
- 2-3 answered positively = WARM LEAD → offer to send more information via SMS
- 1 or fewer = COLD LEAD → thank them and end the call politely

Always log the outcome before ending the call.
```

### 3. Create the Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns → New Campaign**
2. Name it: `Lead Qualification — [Month/Year]`
3. Select your lead-qualification agent
4. Upload your leads CSV (columns: `first_name`, `last_name`, `phone`, `email`, `source`)
5. Set calling window: **Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm**
6. Set retries: **2 attempts, 45-minute interval**
7. Launch or schedule

### 4. Configure Post-Call Automations

For **HOT LEADS**: Trigger appointment booking flow (see [Appointment Booking](/campaigns/appointment-booking)) For **WARM LEADS**: Send SMS with your brochure link For **COLD LEADS**: Update CRM status to `Unqualified` and exclude from future campaigns

## Monitoring Results

After the campaign runs:

1. Go to **Campaigns →** your campaign **→ Results**
2. View breakdown by outcome: Connected, No Answer, Qualified, Unqualified
3. Click any call to read the full transcript
4. Export results as CSV for CRM import
