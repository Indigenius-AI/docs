# Campaign Workflows

Campaigns are how you deploy your Indigenius agent **at scale**. Instead of waiting for calls to come in, campaigns proactively dial a list of contacts and work through structured conversation flows.

---

## What Is a Campaign?

A campaign combines:

- A **contact list** (who to call)
- An **agent** (who makes the call)
- A **phone number** (what number to call from)
- A **workflow** (what happens during and after the call)
- A **schedule** (when to make the calls)

---

## Campaign Types

| Campaign Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Lead Qualification** | Call leads and score them based on their responses |
| **Appointment Booking** | Call prospects and schedule meetings directly on the call |
| **Customer Follow-Up** | Re-engage existing customers with updates or offers |
| **SMS Follow-Up** | Send SMS messages after a call or trigger event |
| **Email Follow-Up** | Send personalised emails after a call outcome |

---

## Creating a Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns → New Campaign**
2. Enter a **Campaign Name**
3. Select your **Agent**
4. Select your **Phone Number** (outbound caller ID)
5. Upload or select your **Contact List**
6. Choose your **Campaign Type**
7. Configure the **Schedule** (start date/time, call window, timezone)
8. Set **Retry Logic** (see [Retry Logic](/phone-setup/retry-logic))
9. Click **Launch Campaign** or **Save as Draft**

---

## Campaign Status

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Created but not yet launched |
| **Scheduled** | Will launch at the configured start time |
| **Active** | Currently making calls |
| **Paused** | Temporarily stopped; can be resumed |
| **Completed** | All contacts attempted; campaign ended |
| **Archived** | Stored for reference; no further action |
