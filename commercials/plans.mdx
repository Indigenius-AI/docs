## Free Trial

The Free Trial gives you everything you need to build and test your first agent:

- **30 minutes** of call time (inbound or outbound)
- **1 AI agent**
- **1 shared phone number** (Nigerian local)
- **Website widget** (embed on your site)
- **Basic call logs**
- No credit card required

<Note>
  Free trial minutes do not roll over. Once 30 minutes are used, you'll need to upgrade to continue.
</Note>

### Upgrade from Free Trial

1. Go to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your desired plan
4. Enter payment details and confirm

## Cool Plan

The **Cool Plan** is for small businesses and solo operators launching their first calling campaigns.

- **500 minutes/month** included
- **3 agents**
- **2 phone numbers** (local or international)
- Outbound campaigns with CSV upload
- SMS follow-up automations
- Standard analytics
- Email support with 48-hour response SLA

**Additional minutes** are billed at ₦50/minute (Nigerian numbers) or \$0.10/minute (US/international).

---

## Prime Plan

The **Prime Plan** is for growing teams running regular campaigns with CRM integration.

- **2,000 minutes/month** included
- **10 agents**
- **5 phone numbers**
- Full CRM integration (Zoho)
- Email follow-up automations
- Calendar integrations for booking
- Advanced analytics with export
- Priority email support with 24-hour SLA

**Additional minutes** billed at a discounted rate.

<Tip>
  Most businesses with an active sales team find the Prime plan covers their daily campaign volume comfortably.
</Tip>

---

## Enterprise Plan

The **Enterprise Plan** is for large organisations, call centres, and businesses with high-volume or highly customised requirements.

- **Custom minute allocation** (negotiated per contract)
- **Unlimited agents**
- **Unlimited phone numbers**
- Whitelabel option (your brand, not Indigenius)
- Custom integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot, SAP, etc.)
- Dedicated infrastructure and uptime SLA (99.9%)
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Dedicated Slack support channel
- Custom contract and invoicing

To discuss Enterprise pricing, contact [support](/support/overview) or email [**enterprise@indigenius.ai**](mailto:enterprise@indigenius.ai).

## Billing & Payments

- Plans are billed **monthly** (no annual lock-in on Cool and Prime)
- Enterprise contracts are annual
- Accepted payment methods: Card (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer (Nigeria), USD wire (international)
- Invoices are emailed on the 1st of each month
- VAT applies to Nigerian customers per FIRS regulations

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change plans mid-month?** Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

**What happens if I run out of minutes?** Calls will pause until you top up or upgrade. You'll receive an email alert when you reach 80% usage.

**Do unused minutes roll over?** No. Included minutes reset at the start of each billing cycle.

**Is there a setup fee?** No setup fees on Cool or Prime. Enterprise contracts may include an onboarding fee depending on scope.
