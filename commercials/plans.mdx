## Free Trial

Explore AI agents, voice & content tools.:

- **5,000 credits**
- All TTS & STT voices
- 10\+ African languages
- Standard dubbing (no lip-sync, max 30s)
- Community support

<Note>
  Free trial minutes do not roll over. Once 5,000 credits are used, you'll need to upgrade to continue.
</Note>

### Upgrade from Free Trial

1. Go to **Billing** in the sidebar
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your desired plan
4. Enter payment details and confirm

## Billing & Payments

- Plans are billed **monthly** (no annual lock-in on Cool and Prime)
- Enterprise contracts are annual
- Accepted payment methods: Card (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer (Nigeria), USD wire (international)
- Invoices are emailed on the 1st of each month
- VAT applies to Nigerian customers per FIRS regulations

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change plans mid-month?** Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

**What happens if I run out of minutes?** Calls will pause until you top up or upgrade. You'll receive an email alert when you reach 80% usage.

**Do unused minutes roll over?** No. Included minutes reset at the start of each billing cycle.

**Is there a setup fee?** No setup fees on Cool or Prime. Enterprise contracts may include an onboarding fee depending on scope.
