# Support

The Indigenius support team is here to help you get set up, resolve issues, and get the most out of the platform. We offer support via **Slack** and **WhatsApp**, with clear response time commitments based on your plan.

---

| Channel | Available To | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Community Forum** | All plans | General questions, tips, peer help |
| **Email Support** | Cool, Prime, Enterprise | Technical issues, billing questions |
| **WhatsApp Support** | Prime, Enterprise | Quick questions, real-time help |
| **Dedicated Slack Channel** | Enterprise only | Priority support, direct team access |
| **Setup Assistance Call** | Prime, Enterprise | Hands-on onboarding and configuration |

## Slack Support

Enterprise customers receive a **dedicated Slack channel** shared between the Indigenius team and your team.

### How it works:

1. After signing up for Enterprise, you'll receive an invitation to join the shared Slack workspace
2. Your dedicated channel is named `#support-[your-company-name]`
3. Tag `@indigenius-support` for urgent issues
4. Use threads for ongoing conversation about specific topics
5. The channel is active **Monday–Friday, 8am–6pm WAT** (West Africa Time)

**Response time SLA (Enterprise Slack):** Within 2 hours during business hours.

---

## WhatsApp Support

Prime and Enterprise customers can reach support via **WhatsApp Business**.

### How to use WhatsApp support:

1. Save the Indigenius support number: **\+234-XXX-XXX-XXXX** _(provided in your welcome email)_
2. Send a WhatsApp message describing your issue
3. Include:
   - Your **account email**
   - A **brief description** of the issue
   - Any **error messages** or **screenshots** if relevant
4. The support team will respond and may ask for screen share or call access

**WhatsApp support hours:** Monday–Saturday, 8am–7pm WAT.

---

## Email Support

All paid plans include email support at [**support@indigenius.ai**](mailto:support@indigenius.ai).

When emailing:

- Use your registered account email
- Include your **account name** and **organisation**
- Describe the issue clearly with steps to reproduce
- Attach screenshots, call IDs, or log excerpts where relevant

## Escalation Paths

If your issue is not resolved within the SLA:

1. **Reply to your open email thread** and add the subject line: `ESCALATION: [Brief Issue Description]`
2. For WhatsApp: message the support number with `ESCALATE` followed by your original issue
3. Enterprise Slack: tag `@indigenius-ops` directly in your channel
4. For critical production outages: email [**urgent@indigenius.ai**](mailto:urgent@indigenius.ai) directly

Escalated issues are reviewed by senior engineers within **30 minutes** on Enterprise.

## Setup Assistance

Need hands-on help getting started? The Indigenius team offers **guided setup sessions**:

### What's included:

- 60-minute video call with a Solutions Engineer
- Live agent prompt review and optimisation
- CRM integration walkthrough and field mapping
- Test call together with real-time feedback
- Post-session summary with action items

### How to request:

1. Go to **Settings → Support → Request Setup Call**
2. Choose your preferred date and time
3. Provide a brief description of what you want to accomplish

Setup assistance sessions are available on **Prime** and **Enterprise** plans. Cool plan customers can purchase an ad-hoc session at a fixed fee.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I report a bug?** Email [**bugs@indigenius.ai**](mailto:bugs@indigenius.ai) with a description of the issue, your account email, the steps to reproduce, and any error messages. Include a call ID from **Logs** if the issue is call-related.

**My agent is not calling. What should I do first?** Check: (1) your phone number is assigned to the agent, (2) you have remaining minutes on your plan, (3) the contact phone number is in E.164 format, and (4) the campaign status is **Active**.

**Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?** Indigenius offers a 7-day money-back guarantee for first-time upgrades from Free Trial. Contact [**billing@indigenius.ai**](mailto:billing@indigenius.ai) within 7 days of upgrading.

**How do I cancel my subscription?** Go to **Billing → Manage Subscription → Cancel**. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period.
