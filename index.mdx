## How Voice Agents Work

Every Indigenius agent combines three core AI technologies in a real-time pipeline:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Speech-To-Text (STT)" color="#2596be" icon="microphone" iconType="solid">
    Audio from the caller is transcribed into text in real time with low-latency accuracy.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Large Language Model (LLM)" color="#2596be" icon="brain" iconType="solid">
    The transcript is processed by an LLM that follows your prompt, knowledge base, and logic rules.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Text-To-Speech (TTS)" color="#2596be" icon="volume-high" iconType="solid">
    The LLM's response is converted to natural speech and delivered back to the caller instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Average turn latency is **~0.75 seconds** — fast enough for natural, uninterrupted conversation.
</Note>
