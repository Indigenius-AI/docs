# Introduction to Indigenius Agent Pro

The developer platform for building voice AI agents.

Indigenius Agent Pro enables you to **build, test, and deploy real-time voice agents** with ease.

It abstracts away the complexity of underlying infrastructure, making it simple and scalable to create **intelligent, production-ready agents**.

---

## Key Capabilities

- **Conversational flow**: Turn-taking, interruptions, and backchanneling
- **Low latency**: ~0.75s between turns
- **Programmable logic**: Tool calls, workflows, widgets, and APIs
- **Telephony and streaming**: SIP and web calls
- **Observability**: Transcripts, call reports, metadata, and logs

Indigenius Agent Pro is a product built on top of voice agents.

---

## What is a Voice Agent?

We call them **Agents**, and they are composed of 3 parts:

<div className="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4 mt-6">

  <div className="p-4 rounded-xl border shadow-sm bg-white hover:border-[#F79320] transition-colors">
    <p className="font-semibold">Speech-To-Text (STT)</p>
    <p className="text-sm text-gray-600">Audio from the user is transcribed</p>
  </div>

  <div className="p-4 rounded-xl border shadow-sm bg-white hover:border-[#F79320] transition-colors">
    <p className="font-semibold">Large-Language-Model (LLM)</p>
    <p className="text-sm text-gray-600">Transcript is fed to an LLM</p>
  </div>

  <div className="p-4 rounded-xl border shadow-sm bg-white hover:border-[#F79320] transition-colors">
    <p className="font-semibold">Text-To-Speech (TTS)</p>
    <p className="text-sm text-gray-600">Outputs from LLM are spoken back to user</p>
  </div>

</div>

Each layer is highly customizable and we support dozens of models across STT, LLM and TTS.
