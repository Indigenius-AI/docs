# Nigerian Numbers

Indigenius provides **local Nigerian numbers** (area-code-based) afor businesses operating in Nigeria.

---

## Nigerian Local Numbers

Local numbers follow the standard Nigerian format: `+234-XXX-XXXX-XXX`. They are ideal for:

- Businesses that want a local presence in specific states or cities
- Outbound campaigns where a local caller ID increases pickup rates
- Inbound support lines where callers pay standard mobile rates

### Purchasing a Local Nigerian Number

1. Go to **Phone Numbers → Add Number**
2. Select **Country: Nigeria**
3. Browse available numbers 
4. Click **Purchase**, the number is active immediately
5. Assign the number to your agent

<Tip>
  Nigerian local numbers in premium areas may have limited availability.
</Tip>

---

## Assigning a Number to an Agent

1. Go to **Phone Numbers** and click on your number
2. Click **Assign Agent**
3. Select the agent from the dropdown
4. Click **Save**

---

## Inbound vs. Outbound Use

| Use | Configuration |
| --- | --- |
| **Inbound only** | Assign agent, set routing type, test by calling in |
| **Outbound only** | Use as caller ID in campaigns — no inbound routing needed |

---

## Compliance Notes

- Nigerian numbers are provisioned in compliance with NCC (Nigerian Communications Commission) regulations
- Numbers must be used for the declared business purpose at time of registration
