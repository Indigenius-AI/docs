# Nigerian Numbers

Indigenius provides **local Nigerian numbers** (area-code-based) and **toll-free numbers** (0800 prefix) for businesses operating in Nigeria.

---

## Nigerian Local Numbers

Local numbers follow the standard Nigerian format: `+234-XXX-XXXX-XXX`. They are ideal for:

- Businesses that want a local presence in specific states or cities
- Outbound campaigns where a local caller ID increases pickup rates
- Inbound support lines where callers pay standard mobile rates

### Purchasing a Local Nigerian Number

1. Go to **Phone Numbers → Buy Number**
2. Select **Country: Nigeria**
3. Select **Type: Local**
4. Browse available numbers or search by prefix
5. Click **Purchase** — the number is active immediately
6. Assign the number to your agent

<Tip>
  Lagos (01), Abuja (09), and Port Harcourt (084) area-code numbers are available. Numbers in premium areas may have limited availability.
</Tip>

---

## Nigerian Toll-Free Numbers (0800)

Toll-free numbers begin with **0800** and are **free for callers** on supported networks. They are ideal for:

- Customer support lines where you want to encourage inbound calls
- Government and NGO programs
- High-volume inbound campaigns

<Warning>
  Toll-free numbers incur a **higher per-minute cost on your account** because you absorb the caller's cost. Review your [pricing plan](/commercials/plans) before purchasing.
</Warning>

### Purchasing a Toll-Free Number

1. Go to **Phone Numbers → Buy Number**
2. Select **Country: Nigeria**
3. Select **Type: Toll-Free**
4. Review available 0800 numbers
5. Click **Purchase** and assign to your agent

---

## Assigning a Number to an Agent

1. Go to **Phone Numbers** and click on your number
2. Click **Assign Agent**
3. Select the agent from the dropdown
4. Choose routing type:
   - **Direct** — all calls go to this agent
   - **Time-Based** — agent handles calls only within defined hours; outside those hours, callers hear a voicemail
5. Click **Save**

---

## Inbound vs. Outbound Use

| Use | Configuration |
| --- | --- |
| **Inbound only** | Assign agent, set routing type, test by calling in |
| **Outbound only** | Use as caller ID in campaigns — no inbound routing needed |
| **Both** | Assign agent for inbound; use as caller ID for outbound campaigns |

---

## Compliance Notes

- Nigerian numbers are provisioned in compliance with NCC (Nigerian Communications Commission) regulations
- Toll-free numbers must not be used for outbound cold-calling campaigns
- Numbers must be used for the declared business purpose at time of registration
