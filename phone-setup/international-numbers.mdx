# US & International Numbers

Indigenius supports phone number provisioning in **30\+ countries**, including the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, South Africa, Ghana, and Kenya.

---

## US Numbers

### Local US Numbers

US local numbers follow the `+1-NXX-NXX-XXXX` format. They are ideal for:

- US-based customer outreach campaigns
- American businesses using Indigenius agents for customer support
- Diaspora-focused Nigerian businesses reaching US audiences

### US Toll-Free Numbers

US toll-free numbers use the `+1-800`, `+1-888`, `+1-877`, or `+1-866` prefixes.

<Warning>
  US toll-free numbers cannot be used for **automated outbound cold calls** (TCPA compliance). They are best used as inbound support lines.
</Warning>

### Purchasing a US Number

1. Go to **Phone Numbers → Buy Number**
2. Set **Country: United States**
3. Choose **Local** or **Toll-Free**
4. Filter by area code if needed (e.g., 212 for New York)
5. Click **Purchase** and assign to your agent

## Non-Toll-Free Considerations

For non-toll-free numbers used in outbound campaigns:

- Callers see your number as the caller ID
- Local numbers increase pickup rates (callers recognise the area code)
- You can rotate between multiple numbers to manage call volume
- If a number receives too many "declined" responses, it may be flagged by carriers as spam — rotate numbers to avoid this

---

## Number Porting

If you have an existing business number you want to keep:

1. Go to **Phone Numbers → Port Number**
2. Enter your current number and carrier details
3. Submit a **Letter of Authorization (LOA)**
4. Porting typically takes **5–10 business days**
