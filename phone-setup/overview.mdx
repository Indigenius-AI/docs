# Phone Setup

Phone numbers are the entry point for all inbound calls and the caller ID for all outbound calls. This section walks you through purchasing numbers, assigning them to agents, and monitoring your calls.

---

## How Phone Numbers Work

```text
Inbound:  Caller dials your number → Indigenius routes to your agent → AI handles the call
Outbound: Campaign triggers dial-out → Indigenius dials contact → AI handles the call
```

---

## Number Types Available

| Type | Best For |
| --- | --- |
| **Nigerian Local Numbers** | Local NG customers, lower per-minute rates |
| **US Numbers** | US-based customers or international expansion |
| **International Numbers** | UK, Canada, South Africa, Ghana, and more |

<Note>
  Numbers are provisioned within **minutes**. Some international numbers may require additional documentation for regulatory compliance.
</Note>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Phone Numbers">
    In the sidebar, click **Phone Numbers** then **Click Add (Top right)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a number">
    Search by country, browse Available Numbers, Select Billing Type, Automate Subscription.** Confirm Selection.**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to an agent">
    After purchase, click **Assign Agent** and select your agent from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload contact">
    Download Sample file, Structure your contacts and save. Upload your contact, select the contact from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule call & monitor">
    Insert call name, pick date, time zone, time and schedule calls. Schele bulk log, monitor call status.
  </Step>
</Steps>
