# Phone Setup

Phone numbers are the entry point for all inbound calls and the caller ID for all outbound calls. This section walks you through purchasing numbers, assigning them to agents, and configuring call handling behaviour.

---

## How Phone Numbers Work

```text
Inbound:  Caller dials your number → Indigenius routes to your agent → AI handles the call
Outbound: Campaign triggers dial-out → Indigenius dials contact → AI handles the call
```

---

## Number Types Available

| Type | Best For |
| --- | --- |
| **Nigerian Local Numbers** | Local NG customers, lower per-minute rates |
| **Nigerian Toll-Free** | Customer-facing support lines (free for callers) |
| **US Numbers** | US-based customers or international expansion |
| **International Numbers** | UK, Canada, South Africa, Ghana, and more |

<Note>
  Nigerian numbers are provisioned within **minutes**. Some international numbers may require additional documentation for regulatory compliance.
</Note>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Phone Numbers">
    In the sidebar, click **Phone Numbers** then **Buy Number**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for a number">
    Filter by country, area code, or number type. Select the number you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to an agent">
    After purchase, click **Assign Agent** and select your agent from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure call handling">
    Set up inbound routing, voicemail, retry logic, and missed-call behaviour.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the number">
    Call the number from your mobile to verify the agent responds correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
